RingBooker Engineering Notes

Voice AI for salons, spas, med spas, clinics, and appointment-based local businesses.

RingBooker is building a voice AI agent that helps salons, spas, med spas, clinics, and other appointment-based businesses capture missed calls, after-hours inquiries, peak-hour overflow, and booking intent without replacing the human team or changing the phone number customers already call.

What we care about

  • Real-time voice AI
  • Phone UX and caller trust
  • Latency and interruption handling
  • Call coverage for salons, spas, med spas, and clinics
  • After-hours call coverage
  • Peak-hour overflow
  • Keeping the current business phone number
  • Human handoff
  • Structured call summaries
  • Voice AI for local businesses
